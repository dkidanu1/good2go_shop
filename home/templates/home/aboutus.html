About GOOD2GO

Good2go is your friendly neighborhood food rescue. We believe that foodwaste is a huge problem and the way to tackle it is to make perfectly good food accessible to the masses at cheaper prices rather than sending it to be disposed of. In 2020 we started Good2Go a local food rescue working with restaurants and supermarkets to rescue produce that is good to eat but legally not able to sit on the shelf anymore. Start your journey to rescuing food with us!
